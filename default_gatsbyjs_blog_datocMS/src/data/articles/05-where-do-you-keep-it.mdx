## Why cleaning your room is so important
Did you know that a clean and tidy bedroom is the secret to happiness? Actually, no it’s not. But it definitely helps and contributes to a happy and healthy lifestyle! If everyday you tiptoe through your mess, stumble over piles of dirty/clean clothes on the floor, find crumbs in and around your bed and spend more than 45 minutes going through your closet just to find what you’re actually looking for, then you might need to tidy up just a lil’ bit.
